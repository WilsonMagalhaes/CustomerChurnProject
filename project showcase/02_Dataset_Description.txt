Dataset Description:
The dataset used for this project is the Telco Customer Churn dataset, sourced from Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/blastchar/telco-customer-churn). 
It contains data on 7,043 customers of a telecom company and includes 21 features. The goal is to predict whether a customer will churn based on their contract type, 
service usage, and charges. Key features include tenure, Contract, MonthlyCharges, TotalCharges, and InternetService, with the target variable being Churn.
